Products: "Queen's Garden" Series

Queen's Garden is a regal collection of exquisite organic spices, aromatic rice, and premium coffee, sourced from the lush fields of India and Sri Lanka to elevate your culinary experiences with unparalleled quality and flavor.

Ceylon Black Peppercorns

Organic black peppercorns from Sri Lanka, bursting with bold, spicy flavor. Our premium peppercorns add depth and heat to any dish.

  • Origin: Sri Lanka
  • 100% Bio / Organic
  • Whole and Ground
  • Glass jar, 100 g. / 400 g.

Ceylon Cinnamon

Organic Ceylon cinnamon from Sri Lanka, known for its sweet, delicate aroma. This "true cinnamon" adds warmth and subtle flavor to both sweet and savory recipes.

  • Origin: Sri Lanka
  • 100% Bio / Organic
  • Sticks and Ground
  • Glass jar, 100 g. / 400 g.

Ceylon Cloves

Organic Ceylon cloves from Sri Lanka, offering intense aromatic power. Our premium cloves bring a robust, slightly sweet flavor to spice blends and baked goods.

  • Origin: Sri Lanka
  • 100% Bio / Organic
  • Grains and Grained
  • Glass jar, 100 g. / 400 g.

Green Kerala Cardamom

Organic green cardamom from Kerala, India, prized for its complex, aromatic flavor. Our fragrant pods add a unique, slightly sweet taste to both savory dishes and desserts.

  • Origin: Kerala, India
  • 100% Bio / Organic
  • Pods and Grained
  • Glass jar, 100 g. / 400 g.

Organic Pusa Basmati

Organic Indian Basmati rice, renowned for its long, slender grains and nutty aroma. Our premium rice creates fluffy, fragrant dishes perfect for various cuisines.

  • Origin: India
  • 100% Bio / Organic
  • Whole Grain (brown) & White
  • Vacuum package: 1 kg. / 5 kg.
